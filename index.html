SangenjaYA House

Your rental house in central Tokyo

History

Sangenjaya House is a beautiful 86-year-old Japanese home with a rich history dating back to its construction in the 1930s. The house has been carefully renovated to offer guests a unique and luxurious experience that seamlessly blends traditional Japanese elements with modern amenities.

Japanese and Scandinavian minimalism

The first floor of Sangenjaya House has been renovated in a luxurious and minimalistic Scandinavian style, offering guests a comfortable and elegant space that is both modern and timeless. The natural materials, and simple yet sophisticated design complements the traditional Japanese elements found throughout the house. Sangenjaya House second floor offers Japanese-style rooms, a peaceful retreat with traditional tatami mats and shoji sliding doors, and guests can enjoy the tranquil neighborhood from the second-floor balcony.
